Refund & Cancellation Policy

At Anamika Jewellers, we ensure the highest quality of our products. Please read our policy carefully:

Cancellation Policy

Orders can be cancelled within 24 hours of placing them, provided they have not been shipped or are not customized items. To cancel, please contact us at +91 9977441399.

Refund & Return Policy

We accept returns only if the product received is damaged, defective, or incorrect. You must inform us within 48 hours of receiving the delivery with an unboxing video.

Once the returned item is received and inspected by our team, we will process your refund. Approved refunds will be credited to the original method of payment within 5-7 working days.

Please note that making charges and custom jewelry orders are non-refundable.
